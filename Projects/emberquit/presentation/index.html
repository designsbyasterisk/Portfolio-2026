Clinical Foundation

THE FAGERSTRÖM METHOD

Measuring Chemical Dependence

Emberquit uses the **Fagerström Test for Nicotine Dependence**, a gold-standard clinical questionnaire, to evaluate the user’s physical addiction level. This assessment runs dynamically during onboarding:

  • Time to first smoke: How quickly the user smokes after waking (strongest indicator of physical dependency).
  • Refraining difficulty: Difficulty staying smoke-free in forbidden areas.
  • Daily volume: Average cigarettes smoked per day.
  • Waking hours: Frequency of smoking in the morning vs. rest of day.

Decay Logic

MATHEMATICAL MODELING

Adaptive Exponential Decay

To reduce withdrawal shocks, the tapering schedule is computed using a **non-linear, exponential ease-out decay curve** rather than a linear reduction. This keeps the early reductions gentle for higher dependence levels.

Cap Calculation Formula: Cap(day) = Baseline * (1 - (day / totalDays))^exp

Where the exponent `exp` shifts based on dependence (High = 1.8, Medium = 1.4, Low = 1.1). A higher exponent creates a flatter curve at the start, granting the brain more time to adapt before stepping down further.

Cravings peak in exactly 4 minutes. Emberquit offers built-in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) tools to bridge this critical window, allowing the user to stay under cap without willpower depletion.

  • Box Breathing: An interactive breathing guide (In 4, Hold 4, Out 4, Hold 4) to suppress autonomic nervous system arousal.
  • Story Library: Deeply engaging, short stories written to distract the mind during the peak 4 minutes of a craving.
  • Companion Sprite: A digital seedling ("Ember") that grows as the user earns XP for beating cravings, creating a visual mascot to protect.
